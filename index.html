JAMIULULOOM,AMBUR. مدرسہ جامع العلوم، آمبور

The Madrasa-e-Jamiul Uloom Ambur (Urdu: مدرسہ جامع العلوم، آمبور‎) is the Islamic Institute which imparts Hifz of Holy Quran to the college and higher secondary students. It is located at Ambur, a town in Vellore district of Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded on 7 May 1998 (Thursday) after asar prayer by Hazrat Aqdas Moulana Qari Habeeb Ahmed Sahib, Hazrat Moulana Obaidullah Asadi Sahib, Hazrat Moulana Gulam Mohammed Wastanvi Sahib, Maulana Mufti Salahuddin Sahib Qasmi and several prominent Islamic scholars (Ulema).

Mufti Salahuddin Sahib Qasmi is the principal of the Madrasa, and Natharsa Mohamed Sayeed Sahib is the correspondent of the Madrasa and Natharsa Nazimuddin sahib is the secretary of this madarsa. And Hafiz Niyaz Sahib, Hafiz Sayeed Sahib and Moulvi abdul qadeer Sahib are the teachers of the Madrasa.

Madrasa-e-Jamiul Uloom,B.A.I.S Program

We at Jamiul uloom are happy to inform you that we are about to launch a special bachelors program (Graduation in Islamic Studies). It is a five years course where a student can learn Aqeedah, Quran, Hadeeth, Fiqh, Tafseer, Arabic, etc., along convention subjects like Accounts, Economics, Commerce, Computer Science, Psychology, etc., This course will be equivalent to any other bachelors program(B.A.). After completing this course a student will be eligible to other masters program like M.B.A, M.A., etc.,


program highlights

  • 5 years Aalimiyat Program
  • English and Urdu Medium
  • Career Oriented Program
  • Course material similar to BA Programs of Darul uloom Deoband, Mazahirul uloom Saharanpur (Darse Nizami)
  • Adherence to the Quran and Sunnah
  • Proficient faculty members from Darul uloom Deoband
  • Arabic Language taught intensively
